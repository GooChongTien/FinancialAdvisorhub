## Introduction

### What is AdvisorHub?

AdvisorHub is a comprehensive insurance advisor platform designed to streamline your daily operations, from lead management to policy sales. The system integrates all essential tools into one unified interface, helping you manage customers, create proposals, track performance, and stay organized.

### Key Benefits

- **All-in-One Platform**: Manage leads, customers, proposals, and policies in one place
- **AI-Powered Assistant**: Mira helps you navigate, analyze data, and complete tasks
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Track your performance and identify opportunities
- **Smart Automation**: Reduce manual work with automated workflows
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Access the system from any device

### System Requirements

- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest version)
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection
- **Screen**: Minimum 1280x720 resolution (desktop recommended)

## Customer Management

### Overview

The Customer module manages both leads (prospects) and clients (customers with policies). It's your central hub for all customer-related activities.

### Lead Management

#### Viewing Leads

1. Navigate to **Customer** from the sidebar
2. The default view shows all leads in a searchable table
3. Use filters to narrow down by:
   - **Status**: Not Contacted, Contacted, Qualified, etc.
   - **Lead Source**: Referral, Social Media, Walk-in, etc.
   - **Date Range**: When the lead was created

#### Adding a New Lead

1. Click **"+ New Lead"** button
2. Fill in required fields:
   - **Name**: Full name of prospect
   - **Contact Number**: Primary phone number
   - **Email**: Email address (optional)
   - **Lead Source**: How you obtained this lead
3. Add optional information:
   - **Notes**: Any relevant context
   - **Preferred Contact**: Best way to reach them
4. Click **"Save Lead"**

#### Lead Workflow Stages

1. **Not Contacted**: New leads awaiting first contact
2. **Contacted**: Initial contact made
3. **Qualified**: Lead shows genuine interest
4. **Proposal**: Proposal created and presented
5. **Negotiation**: Discussing terms
6. **Won**: Converted to customer
7. **Lost**: Lead did not convert (reason tracked)

#### Managing Leads

**To update a lead:**
1. Click on the lead name to open details
2. Update information as needed
3. Change status to reflect current stage
4. Add notes about interactions
5. Click **"Save"**

**To convert a lead:**
1. Open lead details
2. Click **"Convert to Customer"**
3. System creates customer record
4. Lead status changes to "Won"

### Client Management

#### Viewing Clients

1. In the Customer module, switch to **"Clients"** tab
2. View all customers with active or past policies
3. Search by name, policy number, or contact info

#### Client Details Page

Access comprehensive customer information:

**Overview Tab:**
- Personal details (name, contact, DOB)
- Customer since date
- Total premium value
- Number of policies

**Portfolio Tab:**
- List of all policies
- Policy status (Active, Lapsed, Pending)
- Premium amounts and payment schedules
- Coverage summaries

**Servicing Tab:**
- Recent service requests
- Policy changes and endorsements
- Claims history
- Document repository

**Gap Analysis Tab:**
- Coverage assessment
- Identified gaps in protection
- Recommended products
- Cross-sell opportunities

#### Adding Events

Track customer interactions:

1. Open customer details
2. Click **"Add Event"**
3. Select event type:
   - Meeting
   - Phone Call
   - Email
   - Service Request
4. Add notes and set follow-up if needed
5. Click **"Save Event"**

### Customer Portfolio Management

#### Viewing Policies

Each customer's portfolio shows:
- **Policy Number**: Unique identifier
- **Product Name**: Type of insurance
- **Status**: Active, Lapsed, Pending
- **Premium**: Payment amount and frequency
- **Coverage**: Sum assured
- **Inception/Expiry Dates**: Policy term

#### Policy Actions

- **View Details**: Click policy to see full information
- **Request Change**: Submit policy modification request
- **Download Documents**: Access policy documents
- **Add Note**: Record policy-related information

## New Business Module

### Overview

The New Business module guides you through the complete sales process from fact-finding to application submission.

### Sales Process Workflow

The module follows a structured workflow:

1. **Fact Finding**: Gather customer information
2. **Financial Needs Analysis (FNA)**: Analyze financial situation
3. **Product Recommendation**: AI-powered product suggestions
4. **Quotation**: Generate detailed quote
5. **Application**: Complete and submit application

### Step 1: Fact Finding

#### Starting a New Proposal

1. Navigate to **New Business**
2. Click **"Start New Proposal"**
3. Select customer or create new lead
4. Begin fact-finding questionnaire

#### Information Categories

**Personal Information:**
- Full name, NRIC/Passport
- Date of birth, gender
- Marital status, occupation
- Contact details

**Family Information:**
- Spouse details
- Number of dependents
- Children's ages and education plans

**Financial Information:**
- Monthly income
- Existing assets (property, savings)
- Current liabilities (loans, debts)
- Monthly expenses

**Insurance Coverage:**
- Existing policies
- Coverage types and amounts
- Premium commitments

**Health & Lifestyle:**
- Medical history
- Smoker status
- Occupation hazards
- Hobbies and activities

#### Tips for Effective Fact Finding

- Complete all required fields (marked with *)
- Be thorough - more data = better recommendations
- Update information if customer circumstances change
- Save progress frequently using **"Save Draft"** button

### Step 2: Financial Needs Analysis (FNA)

#### Understanding FNA

The FNA calculates the customer's insurance needs based on:
- Income replacement needs
- Outstanding debts
- Dependent care requirements
- Education funding
- Retirement planning
- Emergency fund

#### Reviewing FNA Results

The system displays:
- **Current Coverage**: Total existing insurance
- **Protection Gap**: Additional coverage needed
- **Breakdown by Need**: Life, health, critical illness, etc.
- **Recommended Coverage**: Suggested protection levels

#### Adjusting Parameters

You can modify:
- Income replacement multiplier
- Education cost estimates
- Debt payoff assumptions
- Emergency fund months

Click **"Recalculate"** to see updated results.

### Step 3: Product Recommendation

#### AI-Powered Recommendations

Mira analyzes the customer profile and suggests:
- Most suitable products
- Coverage amounts
- Policy terms
- Premium estimates

#### Reviewing Recommendations

Each recommendation shows:
- **Product Name**: Insurance product type
- **Why Recommended**: Explanation of fit
- **Coverage**: Proposed sum assured
- **Premium**: Estimated cost
- **Confidence Score**: AI certainty level

#### Customizing Recommendations

1. Click **"Adjust"** on any recommendation
2. Modify coverage amount or term
3. System recalculates premium
4. Add or remove products as needed
5. Click **"Confirm Selection"**

### Step 4: Quotation Generation

#### Creating a Quote

1. Review selected products and coverage
2. Click **"Generate Quotation"**
3. System calculates premiums using latest rates
4. Quote includes:
   - Product details
   - Coverage benefits
   - Premium breakdown
   - Payment options
   - Terms and conditions

#### Quotation Options

**Presentation Mode:**
- Professional quote layout
- Customer-friendly language
- Visual comparison charts

**Export Options:**
- PDF for email/print
- Link for digital sharing
- Add to proposal document

#### Quote Adjustments

- Modify coverage amounts
- Change payment frequency
- Add/remove riders
- Apply discounts if applicable

### Step 5: Application Processing

#### Completing the Application

1. Click **"Proceed to Application"**
2. Verify all customer information
3. Complete required fields:
   - Beneficiary details
   - Payment method
   - Medical declarations
   - Consent forms

#### Document Upload

Upload required documents:
- NRIC/Passport copy
- Income proof
- Medical reports (if required)
- Bank account details

#### Submission

1. Review complete application
2. Obtain customer signature (digital or physical)
3. Click **"Submit Application"**
4. System sends to underwriting
5. Receive submission confirmation

#### Tracking Application Status

Monitor progress in **"My Applications"**:
- Pending Review
- Underwriting
- Medical Required
- Approved
- Issued
- Declined

## Mira AI Assistant

### Overview

Mira is your intelligent AI assistant, designed to help you navigate the system, analyze data, complete tasks, and get answers quickly. Available 24/7, Mira understands natural language and can perform actions on your behalf.

### Getting Started with Mira

#### Opening Mira

**Three ways to access:**
1. **Chat Icon**: Click the floating chat button (bottom-right)
2. **Keyboard Shortcut**: Press `Ctrl+K` (Windows) or `Cmd+K` (Mac)
3. **Menu**: Select from quick actions menu

#### Chat Interface

Mira offers two viewing modes:

**Full Screen Mode:**
- Dedicated chat page
- Large conversation area
- Perfect for extended interactions
- Suggested starter prompts

**Split View Mode:**
- Chat panel alongside your work
- Multitask while chatting
- Resizable panel
- Minimizable when not needed

### What Mira Can Do

#### Navigation Assistance

**Example prompts:**
- "Show me my top customers"
- "Open John Smith's customer profile"
- "Go to analytics dashboard"
- "Create a new lead"

Mira will:
- Navigate to the requested page
- Open specific records
- Launch modules
- Apply filters automatically

#### Data Analysis

**Example prompts:**
- "What are my sales for this month?"
- "Show me overdue tasks"
- "Which products am I selling most?"
- "Compare my Q1 and Q2 performance"

Mira will:
- Retrieve relevant data
- Perform calculations
- Generate visualizations
- Provide insights

#### Task Execution

**Example prompts:**
- "Add a follow-up task for tomorrow with John"
- "Schedule a meeting with Sarah next Tuesday"
- "Create a quotation for term life insurance"
- "Search for leads from last week"

Mira will:
- Execute actions
- Fill in forms
- Create records
- Update information

#### Information Retrieval

**Example prompts:**
- "What is the premium for a $500k life policy?"
- "Show me product details for Critical Care Plus"
- "When is my next appointment?"
- "List my pending proposals"

Mira will:
- Search databases
- Present information
- Answer questions
- Provide recommendations

### Interaction Modes

#### Command Mode

**For quick tasks:**
- Direct instructions
- Single-purpose queries
- Fast execution
- Minimal interaction

**Best for:**
- Navigation
- Simple lookups
- Quick actions
- Status checks

#### Copilot Mode

**For guided workflows:**
- Multi-step processes
- Suggestions and recommendations
- Contextual help
- Action confirmations

**Best for:**
- Creating proposals
- Complex analysis
- Decision support
- Learning new features

#### Insight Mode

**For strategic thinking:**
- Pattern recognition
- Trend analysis
- Opportunity identification
- Performance coaching

**Best for:**
- Business review
- Goal planning
- Strategy sessions
- Performance improvement

### Using Mira Effectively

#### Asking Questions

**Tips for clear communication:**

✅ **Good prompts:**
- "Show me customers with premium over $10,000"
- "Create a quotation for Jane Doe for $500k term life"
- "What's my conversion rate this quarter?"

❌ **Unclear prompts:**
- "Show me stuff"
- "What's happening?"
- "Help"

**Be specific:**
- Include names, dates, amounts
- Specify what you want to see
- Mention any constraints or filters

#### Clarifications

If Mira needs more information:
- She'll ask clarifying questions
- Choose from suggested options
- Or provide additional details

**Example:**
```
You: "Show me my customers"
Mira: "Would you like to see:
      1. All customers
      2. Customers added this month
      3. High-value customers
      4. Customers needing follow-up"
```

#### Confirmations

For actions that make changes:
- Mira asks for confirmation
- Review the planned action
- Approve or modify
- Cancel if needed

**Example:**
```
Mira: "I'll create a task:
       - Title: Follow up with John Smith
       - Due: Tomorrow at 10 AM
       - Priority: High

       Should I proceed?"
```

### Starter Prompts

When you first open Mira, you'll see helpful starter prompts:

#### Customer Analysis
"Show me my top customers by premium value"

#### Sales Performance
"What are my sales trends for this quarter?"

#### Pending Tasks
"Show me my pending tasks and upcoming appointments"

#### Recommendations
"Give me product recommendations for my customer base"

### Advanced Features

#### Context Awareness

Mira remembers:
- Your current page
- Previously selected customer
- Recent searches
- Ongoing conversation

This enables natural follow-ups:
```
You: "Show me John Smith's profile"
Mira: [Opens profile]
You: "What policies does he have?"
Mira: [Shows John's policies, knowing context]
```

#### Action Execution

Mira can perform actions:
- Navigate pages
- Fill forms
- Create records
- Update data
- Generate reports
- Send notifications

#### Confidence Indicators

Mira shows confidence levels:
- **High confidence** (80%+): Green badge
- **Medium confidence** (50-80%): Yellow badge
- **Low confidence** (<50%): Orange badge, asks for clarification

#### Intent Recognition

Mira understands various ways to ask:
- "Show my customers" = "List all customers" = "Customer list"
- "Sales this month" = "Monthly revenue" = "How much did I sell?"
- "Add task" = "Create to-do" = "Remind me to..."

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Ctrl+K` / `Cmd+K`: Open/close Mira
- `Esc`: Close chat panel
- `Enter`: Send message
- `Shift+Enter`: New line in message

### Tips & Best Practices

1. **Start simple**: Begin with basic navigation and lookups
2. **Learn by doing**: Try different types of prompts
3. **Use natural language**: Write as you would speak
4. **Provide context**: Include relevant details
5. **Confirm understanding**: Verify Mira's interpretation

### Glossary of Terms

**Lead**: A potential customer who has shown interest but has not yet purchased

**Client/Customer**: A person who has purchased at least one policy

**Prospect**: Alternative term for lead

**Pipeline**: The sales funnel from lead to customer

**FNA**: Financial Needs Analysis - assessment of insurance requirements

**Sum Assured**: The coverage amount or death benefit of a policy

**Premium**: The payment amount for insurance coverage

**Rider**: Optional additional coverage attached to a base policy

**Underwriting**: The process of evaluating and approving an insurance application

**Conversion Rate**: Percentage of leads that become customers

**Cross-sell**: Selling additional products to existing customers

**Policy Servicing**: Making changes or updates to an existing policy
